About i2i CONNECT

i2i CONNECT is a cloud-based Tele diagnostics platform for remote clinical screening/diagnosis and reporting by experts. Medical images and patient data can be uploaded from screening centers and clinics that do not have / prefer not to have resident specialists. Expert panel of specialists provide diagnostic reports online. Physicians / Specialists can access patients, and study images & reports for treatment.
